Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started?+
Just drop us a message on WhatsApp or fill in the contact form. We'll schedule a free 30-minute discovery call to understand your project, then send you a detailed proposal and quote.
How do I pay? What payment methods are accepted?+
We accept bank transfer (BCA, Mandiri, BRI), GoPay, OVO, and QRIS. We require a 50% deposit upfront and the remaining 50% upon final approval before launch.
Can I see a mockup before paying?+
Yes! We provide a design concept / mockup after the 50% deposit. If you're not satisfied with the initial concept, we'll revise it at no extra cost. We don't release the full payment until you're happy.
What if I already have a domain and hosting?+
No problem. We can deploy to your existing domain and hosting. If you don't have one yet, we can guide you on the best options and handle the entire setup for a small additional fee.
Will I be able to update the website myself after launch?+
For Professional and Premium packages, yes — we include a CMS (Content Management System) that lets you update text, images, and blog posts without touching any code. We'll give you a walkthrough video too.
Do you work with clients outside Jakarta?+
Absolutely. We work with clients all across Indonesia and internationally. All communication is done via WhatsApp, Zoom, or email — location is no barrier.
What if I need changes after the site is live?+
Every package includes 14–30 days of post-launch support. After that, you can subscribe to our Monthly Maintenance add-on, or hire us for individual change requests at our standard hourly rate.

Still unsure which package is right for you?
